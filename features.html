Audi R8 LMS

The Audi R8 LMS proves its versatility in worldwide GT3 competition and the second evolution of the race car takes this even further, the new for 2022 Audi R8 LMS GT3 evo II.

Latest evolution optimized in five areas: aerodynamics, engine characteristics, air conditioning, suspension and traction control

Advanced race technology for overall victories in sprint and endurance races

Audi RS e-tron GT

Everything in the Audi RS e-tron GT * is focused on the functional center of the interior - the driver's seat.

The sculptural design combines the dynamic proportions of a Gran Turismo with the unmistakable characteristics of an Audi RS model.

The optional carbon roof consists of carbon fiber reinforced plastic. The high-tech material is very stable and at the same time very light.

Thanks to intelligent thermal management, the performance remains at a rousing level even with multiple sprints * completed in a row.

Audi Q4 Sportback e-tron

The clear design of the Audi Q4 Sportback e-tron gives the future of electric mobility a characteristic shape. With a focused vehicle front and powerful rear.

The progressive interior of the Audi Q4 Sportback e-tron is characterized by technological innovations, sportiness and functionality.

The optional sports suspension with more dynamic spring and damper tuning and lowering by 15 mm offers more direct contact with the road.
